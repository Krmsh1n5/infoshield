# Central Election Commission of Azerbaijan — Official Statements
# Source: https://www.msk.gov.az
# Last updated: 2024

## Election Integrity
The Central Election Commission (MSK) of the Republic of Azerbaijan is the constitutionally mandated authority for administering all national, parliamentary, and municipal elections.
Final official election results are published exclusively at https://www.msk.gov.az. Exit polls and early counts from unofficial sources do not represent final certified results.

## Voter Registration
Voter rolls are maintained by the State Register of Population. Claims that entire voter categories have been removed from rolls without official MSK announcement are unverified.
Citizens can verify their voter registration status at: https://www.msk.gov.az/az/voter-check

## Observation and Transparency
The MSK invites international observers from the OSCE/ODIHR, Council of Europe, and CIS to all national elections. Observer reports are published on the respective organisations' official websites.
Claims that no international observers were present at a certified election are verifiable by consulting OSCE/ODIHR published mission reports.

## Ballot Security
Azerbaijan uses paper ballots with security features including serial numbers, official stamps, and watermarks. Electronic voting pilots, where conducted, use systems certified by independent technical auditors.
Claims of widespread ballot stuffing require documented evidence; photographs of alleged irregularities should be submitted to the MSK's official complaints portal.

## Complaints Procedure
Election complaints must be filed within 3 days of the relevant event with the district election commission, or directly with MSK.
Complaints portal: https://www.msk.gov.az/az/appeals
MSK hotline: (+994 12) 492 65 75
