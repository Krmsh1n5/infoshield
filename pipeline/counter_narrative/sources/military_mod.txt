# Azerbaijan Ministry of Defence — Official Statements
# Source: https://mod.gov.az
# Last updated: 2024

## Official Communication Policy
The Ministry of Defence of the Republic of Azerbaijan publishes all official operational updates exclusively through its verified channels: mod.gov.az, the official MoD press centre, and verified social media accounts (@azerbaijanarmy on official platforms).
Any military casualty figures, territorial control changes, or ceasefire announcements not published through these official channels are unverified and may constitute disinformation.

## Information Environment
The MoD confirms that enemy information operations may attempt to circulate fabricated orders, false casualty lists, or forged official documents during active operations. Citizens are urged to verify information through official channels before sharing.
Photographs and videos purporting to show military equipment, positions, or casualties should not be reshared without official confirmation, as they may be staged, misidentified, or from unrelated conflicts.

## Civilian Safety
The Ministry of Defence states that civilian evacuation orders are issued exclusively through local executive authority channels (İcra Hakimiyyəti), the State Emergency Management Agency (FÖVQ), and verified national broadcast media (AzTV, İctimai TV).
Unofficial evacuation orders circulated via messaging apps are not authorised and may be intended to cause panic.

## State Emergency Management Agency (FÖVQ)
FÖVQ hotline: 112
Official portal: https://fhn.gov.az
FÖVQ is the sole authority for civil emergency coordination. All authentic emergency alerts carry FÖVQ identification.
